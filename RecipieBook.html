Sushi

  • Time: 1H
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
Noori 5 Sheets
Sushi Rice 2 Cups
Tuna 2 Ounces
Cumumber 1
Carrot 1

Step 1: Chop everything but the Noori into decent sized portios.

Step 2: Carefull not to overfill, put it onto the noori and gentley start rolling.

Step 3: Roll and cut into decent sized portions. Good job you are done!

Shish Kabobs

  • Time: 1H
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
Soy Sauce 1/4 Cup
Olive Oil 3/4 Cup
Tomatoes 6
Mushrooms 12
Onions 2

Step 1: Cut everything into bite sized sizes

Step 2: Stick onto a skewer stick

Step 3: Put into a barbeque for 20 mins not on the direct heat

Step 4: Take out, and either eat off the skwer or put onto paltes. Enjoy!

Source: Shish Kabob website

Croissant

  • Time: 1H
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
croissant mix 1 box
flour handfull
egg wash 1 egg

Step 1: Comibine water with croissant mix

Step 2: Spread flour and roll out and cut into triangles

Step 3: roll triangles and put on baking tray while putting eggwash on top.

Step 4: Put into 350 farenheight oven for 20 minutes, and take out and eat.
